WORKFLOW

Three steps to unbreakable security

Get up and running in under two minutes. No complex setup, no cloud accounts, no tracking.

01

Install the Extension

Add SecureVault to Firefox from about:debugging (development) or the Add-ons page. The extension integrates seamlessly into your browser toolbar.

02

Register & Create Master Password

Create your account with a strong master password. This is the only password you'll ever need to remember — it never leaves your device.

03

Browse & Save Securely

Visit any website and log in as usual. SecureVault captures your credentials, encrypts them with your unique key, and stores them in your personal vault.
