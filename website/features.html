CAPABILITIES

Everything you need to stay secure

Built from the ground up with a zero-trust architecture. Every feature is designed to keep your credentials completely private.

🔐

SHA-256 Hashing

Every password is hashed with SHA-256 combined with a unique per-user salt before being stored. No plaintext passwords ever reach the database — not even once.

✨

Smart Auto-Fill

Content scripts intelligently detect login forms and auto-fill your saved credentials. Captures new passwords on form submission with a sleek in-page notification.

🗄️

Secure Vault

Browse, reveal, and manage your saved credentials from the popup vault. Each entry is securely stored and retrieved through the protected backend.

🔑

Account Recovery

Forgot your master password? Recover access through your encrypted safety question and answer. Reset your master password with a time-limited recovery token.

🌐

SSO Web Portal

Log in from any device via the web portal to issue a single-sign-on code. Enter the code in the extension to unlock your vault without typing your master password.

☁️

Cloud Sync

Optionally sync hashed copies of your vault entries to MongoDB Atlas. Your credentials are always hashed — the cloud only stores non-reversible data.
